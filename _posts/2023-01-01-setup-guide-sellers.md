<p>This guide is for <strong>sellers and non-admin users</strong>. It only covers the steps needed to open Meddicc Score inside HubSpot, connect your user if prompted, and use it in your daily workflow.</p>

<p>If you need help with <strong>card placement, permissions, licences, framework settings, automations, or account configuration</strong>, please contact your Admin and use the <a href="{% post_url 2023-01-01-setup-guide %}">full setup guide</a>.</p>

<h4 class="pt-6-m mb-3 text-primary" id="open-the-card">1. Open the Card in HubSpot</h4>

<p>Once your Admin adds the Meddicc Score card to your HubSpot record view, it appears inside your records.</p>

<p>The app may appear in these locations:</p>

<ul>
  <li><strong>Deal:</strong> middle column, right sidebar, and Deal Execution</li>
  <li><strong>Contact:</strong> middle column and right sidebar</li>
  <li><strong>Company:</strong> middle column and right sidebar</li>
</ul>

<p>If you open a record and do not see the card, ask your Admin to add the Meddicc Score card to that view.</p>

<p class="text-center"><img src="/assets/images/guide3n.png" alt="Meddicc Score inside HubSpot" class="my-3 border border-3 border-primary rounded rounded-3"></p>

<p>If the card asks you to finish connecting the app, click the button shown on the card and complete the permission step.</p>

<h4 class="pt-6-m mb-3 text-primary" id="deal-cards">2. Use Deal Cards to Score Opportunities</h4>

<ul>
  <li>The first time you open a Deal, the default framework selected by your Admin is used.</li>
  <li>Click <strong>Update Score</strong> or the pencil icon to open the form.</li>
  <li>Meddicc Score reviews recent deal activity in HubSpot, such as emails, meetings, calls, tasks, notes, and core deal data, to pre-fill the framework responses.</li>
  <li>You can review and edit the suggested answers before saving.</li>
</ul>

<p class="text-center"><img src="/assets/images/guide61n.png" alt="Update Score form" class="my-3 border border-3 border-primary rounded rounded-3"></p>

<ul>
  <li>Click <strong>Refill</strong> if new information has been added to the deal and you want AI to refresh the responses.</li>
  <li>Lock individual responses if you do not want them overwritten during a refill.</li>
  <li>Click <strong>Score</strong> to recalculate the score after updating the form.</li>
</ul>

<h4 class="pt-6-m mb-3 text-primary" id="next-steps">3. Use Next Steps and Analysis</h4>

<ul>
  <li>After scoring, Meddicc Score suggests <strong>Next Steps</strong> and follow-up questions to help you move the deal forward.</li>
  <li>For closed deals, it can show a <strong>Win Analysis</strong> or <strong>Post-Mortem Analysis</strong>.</li>
  <li>You can also send an email, schedule a meeting, or reopen the form from the deal card.</li>
</ul>

<p class="text-center"><img src="/assets/images/guide42.png" alt="Next steps and analysis" class="my-3 border border-3 border-primary rounded rounded-3"></p>

<h4 class="pt-6-m mb-3 text-primary" id="contact-company-cards">4. Use Contact and Company Cards</h4>

<p><strong>Contact cards</strong> help you prepare your next interaction with a contact:</p>

<ul>
  <li>See the deals where that contact is involved.</li>
  <li>Review an AI-generated action plan and talking points.</li>
  <li>Draft an email or schedule a meeting from the card.</li>
</ul>

<p class="text-center"><img src="/assets/images/guide52n.png" alt="Contact card" class="my-3 border border-3 border-primary rounded rounded-3"></p>

<p><strong>Company cards</strong> give you a broader account view:</p>

<ul>
  <li>See related deals and contacts for that company.</li>
  <li>Open deal forms, review contact plans, send emails, or schedule meetings from one place.</li>
</ul>

<p class="text-center"><img src="/assets/images/guide53n.png" alt="Company card" class="my-3 border border-3 border-primary rounded rounded-3"></p>

<hr>

<h4 class="pt-6-m mb-3 text-primary" id="org-charts">5. Optional Tools: Org Charts and Competitor Charts</h4>

<p>If your team uses them, you can open these tools from the Deal form:</p>

<ul>
  <li><strong>Org Chart:</strong> map stakeholders, reporting lines, and buying roles such as Economic Buyer or Champion.</li>
  <li><strong>Competitor Charts:</strong> compare your position against competitors for a specific opportunity.</li>
</ul>

<p class="text-center"><img src="/assets/images/guide611.png" alt="Org chart" class="my-3 border border-3 border-primary rounded rounded-3"></p>

<p class="text-center"><img src="/assets/images/features61.png" alt="Competitor chart" class="my-3 border border-3 border-primary rounded rounded-3"></p>

<hr>

<h4 class="pt-6-m mb-3 text-primary" id="change-framework">6. Change the Framework (If Enabled)</h4>

<ul>
  <li>Your Admin can allow sellers to switch frameworks for a specific deal.</li>
  <li>If enabled, you can choose a different framework and AI will pre-fill it using the deal data.</li>
  <li>Changing the framework does not automatically rescore or overwrite previous saved answers.</li>
</ul>

<p class="text-center"><img src="/assets/images/guide4.png" alt="Change framework" class="my-3 border border-3 border-primary rounded rounded-3"></p>

<hr>

<h4 class="pt-6-m mb-3 text-primary" id="reporting">7. Access the Report (If Enabled)</h4>

<ul>
  <li>Open the Meddicc Score home screen from the HubSpot marketplace icon.</li>
  <li>If your Admin has enabled report access for non-admin users, you can review your deals and score trends there.</li>
  <li>Admins may have access to broader team reporting than standard sellers.</li>
</ul>

<p class="text-center"><img src="/assets/images/guide98n2.png" alt="Reporting dashboard" class="my-3 border border-3 border-primary rounded rounded-3"></p>

<h3 class="pt-6-m mb-3 text-primary" id="ask-admin">When to Ask Your Admin for Help</h3>

<ul>
  <li>You do not see the Meddicc Score card, or the card does not let you finish connecting your user.</li>
  <li>You need access to change frameworks, edit scores manually, or view reports.</li>
  <li>You need changes to settings, users, licences, automations, or the default framework.</li>
</ul>
